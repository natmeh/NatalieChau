Financial Literacy in School

Most Effective Method:

Jump$tart Financial Foundations for Educators is a program for teachers. It saves time for teachers since the slides that the teachers use to learn are similar to the slides they present to their students.
The most effective way to learn financial literacy is to teach teacher show to teach financial literacy through the Jump$tart program.
Through this resource, teachers will learn to teach about Saving/Spending, Investment, Managing Credit, Debt and Identity Theft, Insurance.

Use of Technology

Through the power of the internet, teachers are able to reach each other online (LinkedIn Certifications).
Technology can be used to emphasize how important financial literacy is. Sharing the impacts that financial literacy has on students will encourage more teachers to do the program as well.
Jump$start conducted study on students of all race, gender, family and economic background. They conducted their studies all online, as follow:
  • Teacher Pre-survey & Student Pre-test in January 2019
  • Personal finance course from January-June 2019
  • Student Post-test April-June 2019
  • J$ Financial Foundations Training June-July 2019
  • Personal finance course September- January 2020
  • Student Post-test & Teacher Post-survey January-February 2020
  • Teacher Focus Group May 2020
  • Follow on, semi-structured, individual teacher interviews February 2021
